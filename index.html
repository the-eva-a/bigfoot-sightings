BFRO Report Classification System

All reports posted into the BFRO's online database are assigned a classification: Class A, Class B, or Class C. The classifications relate to the potential for misinterpretation of what was observed or heard.

Class A

Reports involve clear sightings where misidentification of other animals can be ruled out with confidence.

Class B

Incidents involve observations in poor conditions or distinct sounds associated with sasquatches. These reports lack clear visuals but are still credible.

Class C

Second or third-hand reports with higher potential for inaccuracy. These are usually archived and rarely displayed publicly.
